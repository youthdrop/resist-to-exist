Why We Are Fighting

Standalone PC 148 charges are not small. They can change someone’s life.

PC 148 is often described as “resisting, delaying, or obstructing” an officer. But when it is filed alone, with no other criminal charge, it raises serious questions about whether the charge is being used to punish people for asserting their rights.

These cases can lead to jail, court dates, missed work, warrants, fear, trauma, legal debt, and public records that follow people long after the police encounter is over.
