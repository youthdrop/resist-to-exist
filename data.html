Key Findings

  • 1,378 standalone PC 148 cases were identified.
  • 970 cases were declined by prosecutors.
  • 70.4% of cases did not move forward.
  • Hispanic residents had the highest raw charge count.
  • Black residents were reported as 9× more likely than White residents to be charged.

Methodology

Data were obtained through public records requests and analyzed using Python and Pandas. This dashboard separates raw counts from population-adjusted disparity findings.

Current fields reviewed include race, booking charge, issuing status, and final disposition.
